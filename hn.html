Niyitanga Honore

    resident of Kayonza district in Rwanda.

        Honore is a responsible and diligent young 
        man who is determined to achieve his goals.

        love for learning. Honore attended primary school in
        his hometown and subsequently enrolled in secondary school,

        In addition to his academic pursuits, Honore is a caring

        his time to help others, whether it is by assisting with

        Honore has big dreams for his future, and he aspires to become
